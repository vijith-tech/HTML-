Maratha Marvels

ne of the world’s oldest occupied cities, Madurai in the ancient times had been under the rule of Chola, Nayak and Pandya dynasties. It even finds its mention in the writings of Kautilya and Megasthenes. Madurai is home to a number of magnificent ancient temples. The city is placed on the fertile banks of Vaigai River and gets divided into two parts, by the same river.The city is known for being a prime center for Tamil culture and learning from around 4000 years ago. Amongst the several impressive temples, The Meenakshi Amman Temple is the most popular one, extremely beautiful. The 12-day Chithirai Festival held during the month of April is the major attraction.

Nellai and Historical

lso known as Udhagamandalam, the popular hill town of Ooty was founded in the first half of 19th century by the British rulers as a summer retreat for the Chennai viceroys. Lying at the foothills of the Nilgiris or the Blue Mountains, Ooty is at its best during the months of April and May. This soothing hill town is perfect for both, family vacationers and newly-wedded couples.The cool breeze, amazing weather, rolling hills, lush mountains, several tourists attractions in Ooty, every year attract a crazy number of tourists from all across the world. Some of the prime attracts include Ooty Lake, Government Botanical Garden, Doddabetta Peak, Toy Train, Kodanadu View Point, Rose Garden and more.
